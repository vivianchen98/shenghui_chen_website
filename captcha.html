Iteratively Refined CAPTCHAs

Category: Project Timeline: Fall 2020 Keywords: Computer Vision

Shenghui Chen, Margaret Cheng, and Sophia Cheung. Iteratively Refined CAPTCHAs Final Project of CS 4501: Vision & language

Approach


We approach the problem with an iterative adversarial architecture. Given a specific CAPTCHA dataset, we first design a CAPTCHA Breaker to attempt to recognize the given CAPTCHAs; if the CAPTCHAs have proven breakable, we then utilize a New CAPTCHA Generator to generate more complicated CAPTCHAS. These two steps are performed iteratively until the CAPTCHAs generated by our model is no longer breakable by the Breaker.
